Researchers at Middlebury College published an estimated 892 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 86 to 131.

Looking only at 2023's output of 128 articles, 53.1% have a Corresponding Author from Middlebury College. Of those, 38.2% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (26 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were The Astrophysical Journal and PLOS ONE.

Corresponding Authored papers from Middlebury College during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
