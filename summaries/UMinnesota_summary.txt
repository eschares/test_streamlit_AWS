Researchers at University of Minnesota published an estimated 63,628 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 7,018 to 8,861.

Looking only at 2023's output of 8,072 articles, 43.7% have a Corresponding Author from University of Minnesota. Of those, 56.6% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (1,994 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were PLOS ONE and Scientific Reports.

Corresponding Authored papers from University of Minnesota during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
