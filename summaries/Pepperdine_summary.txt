Researchers at **Pepperdine University** published an estimated 853 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 63 to 155.

Looking only at 2023's output of 149 articles, 46.3% have a Corresponding Author from Pepperdine University. Of those, 8.7% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (6 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Body Image* and *Frontiers in Marine Science*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Pepperdine University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
