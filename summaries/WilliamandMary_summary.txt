Researchers at William & Mary published an estimated 4,769 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 544 to 675.

Looking only at 2023's output of 557 articles, 44.9% have a Corresponding Author from William & Mary. Of those, 45.6% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (114 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were Estuaries and Coasts and PLOS ONE.

Corresponding Authored papers from William & Mary during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Commerce.
