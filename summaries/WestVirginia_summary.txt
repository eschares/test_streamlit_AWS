Researchers at **West Virginia University** published an estimated 15,921 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 1,649 to 2,284.

Looking only at 2023's output of 2,193 articles, 48.3% have a Corresponding Author from West Virginia University. Of those, 47.4% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (502 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *PLOS ONE* and *The Astrophysical Journal*.

Corresponding Authored papers from West Virginia University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
