Researchers at *Cary Institute of Ecosystem Studies* published an estimated 699 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 65 to 129.

Looking only at 2023's output of 65 articles, 29.2% have a Corresponding Author from Cary Institute of Ecosystem Studies. Of those, 84.2% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (16 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were Ecosphere and Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America.

Corresponding Authored papers from Cary Institute of Ecosystem Studies during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Agriculture.
