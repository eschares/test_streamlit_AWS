Researchers at SUNY Geneseo published an estimated 419 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 37 to 72.

Looking only at 2023's output of 37 articles, 45.9% have a Corresponding Author from SUNY Geneseo. Of those, 35.3% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (6 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were Acta Crystallographica Section E: Cr and Acta Crystallographica Section C: St.

Corresponding Authored papers from SUNY Geneseo during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
