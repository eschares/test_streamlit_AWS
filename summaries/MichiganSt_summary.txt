Researchers at **Michigan State University** published an estimated 44,890 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 4,761 to 6,342.

Looking only at 2023's output of 5,770 articles, 41.9% have a Corresponding Author from Michigan State University. Of those, 51.2% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (1,238 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *PLOS ONE* and *Scientific Reports*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Michigan State University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
