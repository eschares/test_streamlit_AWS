Researchers at **SUNY Brockport** published an estimated 823 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 84 to 127.

Looking only at 2023's output of 100 articles, 46.0% have a Corresponding Author from SUNY Brockport. Of those, 21.7% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (10 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Journal of Great Lakes Research* and *Wetlands*.

Corresponding Authored papers from SUNY Brockport during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Commerce and National Science Foundation.
