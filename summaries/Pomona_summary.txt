Researchers at **Pomona College** published an estimated 856 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 78 to 131.

Looking only at 2023's output of 114 articles, 34.2% have a Corresponding Author from Pomona College. Of those, 23.1% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (9 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Journal of Number Theory* and *Journal of Inorganic Biochemistry*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Pomona College during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
