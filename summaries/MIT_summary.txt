Researchers at **Massachusetts Institute of Technology** published an estimated 51,495 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 5,994 to 6,995.

Looking only at 2023's output of 6,469 articles, 37.7% have a Corresponding Author from Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Of those, 62.2% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (1,517 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Nature Communications* and *Proceedings of the National Academy*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Massachusetts Institute of Technology during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
