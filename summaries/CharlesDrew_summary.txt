Researchers at **Charles R. Drew University of Medicine and Science** published an estimated 788 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 52 to 163.

Looking only at 2023's output of 116 articles, 49.1% have a Corresponding Author from Charles R. Drew University of Medicine and Science. Of those, 64.9% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (37 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *International Journal of Environmental Research* and *Public Health and Brain Sciences*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Charles R. Drew University of Medicine and Science during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and United States Department of Defense.
