Researchers at Iowa State University published an estimated 23,548 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 2,744 to 3,143.

Looking only at 2023's output of 2,776 articles, 48.1% have a Corresponding Author from Iowa State University. Of those, 54.9% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (733 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were PLOS ONE and Journal of Animal Science.

Corresponding Authored papers from Iowa State University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Agriculture.
