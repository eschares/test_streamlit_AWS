Researchers at Indiana University Bloomington published an estimated 17,000 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 1,835 to 2,459.

Looking only at 2023's output of 2,184 articles, 43.5% have a Corresponding Author from Indiana University Bloomington. Of those, 50.3% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (477 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were Journal of the American Chemical Soc and Proceedings of the National Academy.

Corresponding Authored papers from Indiana University Bloomington during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
