Researchers at **Pennsylvania State University** published an estimated 56,211 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 6,007 to 8,018.

Looking only at 2023's output of 7,371 articles, 46.2% have a Corresponding Author from Pennsylvania State University. Of those, 56.1% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (1,912 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Nature Communications* and *Scientific Reports*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Pennsylvania State University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
