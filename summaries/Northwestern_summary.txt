Researchers at **Northwestern University** published an estimated 54,104 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 5,757 to 7,556.

Looking only at 2023's output of 7,161 articles, 43.1% have a Corresponding Author from Northwestern University. Of those, 62.5% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (1,927 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Journal of the American Chemical Soc* and *Nature Communications*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Northwestern University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
