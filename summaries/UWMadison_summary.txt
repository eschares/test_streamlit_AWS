Researchers at **University of Wisconsin Madison** published an estimated 56,707 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 6,481 to 7,804.

Looking only at 2023's output of 7,656 articles, 45.6% have a Corresponding Author from University of Wisconsin Madison. Of those, 63.6% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (2,218 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *PLOS ONE* and *Proceedings of the National Academy*.

Corresponding Authored papers from University of Wisconsin Madison during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
