Researchers at **Dartmouth College** published an estimated 13,529 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 1,523 to 1,942.

Looking only at 2023's output of 1,806 articles, 40.1% have a Corresponding Author from Dartmouth College. Of those, 62.3% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (452 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Scientific Reports* and *Journal of Geophysical Research Spac*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Dartmouth College during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
