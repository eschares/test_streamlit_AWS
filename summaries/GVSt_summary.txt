Researchers at Grand Valley State University published an estimated 1,795 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 184 to 235.

Looking only at 2023's output of 234 articles, 47.4% have a Corresponding Author from Grand Valley State University. Of those, 24.3% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (27 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were Acta Crystallographica Section E: Cr and Journal of Great Lakes Research.

Corresponding Authored papers from Grand Valley State University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies National Science Foundation and United States Department of Health and Human Services.
