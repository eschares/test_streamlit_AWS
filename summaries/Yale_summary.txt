Researchers at **Yale University** published an estimated 65,695 articles over the eight years 2016-2023, with a single year's output ranging from 6,744 to 9,360.

Looking only at 2023's output of 9,360 articles, 43.1% have a Corresponding Author from Yale University. Of those, 61.6% acknowledge some form of US federal funding (2,484 total articles).

The top journal destinations for these Corresponding Authored and US federally funded articles across all years were *Proceedings of the National Academy* and *Nature Communications*.

Corresponding Authored papers from Yale University during the timeframe studied here most commonly acknowledged the US federal funding agencies United States Department of Health and Human Services and National Science Foundation.
